All Users i

Flags: approve users — can approve/deny pending requests. approve admins — can grant "approve users" to others. grant sudo — can grant all permissions including itself. Click a flag to toggle. Providers: OAuth login methods linked to this account. × to unlink (cannot remove last one). Merge: combine two accounts — provider links move to the target, source is deleted. Deactivate: soft-delete — renames account to cpx-, removes logins, preserves data.
